About This Portal

Official Technical Commission Notice Board

This portal is maintained by the AFNM Technical Commission. It serves as the official digital notice board for all FIA CEZ events organised under AFNM. All bulletins, supplementary regulations, entry lists, and official communications are published here.

FIA CEZ Championship

The FIA Central European Zone Championship covers circuit racing across Central and Eastern Europe. AFNM oversees competitors representing North Macedonia and is responsible for technical scrutineering, officiating, and administrative functions at designated events.

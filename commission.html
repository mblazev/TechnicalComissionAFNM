About the Commission

Role & Mandate

The Technical Commission of the Automobile Federation of North Macedonia (AFNM) is the body responsible for overseeing all technical and sporting matters at FIA-sanctioned events organised under the AFNM banner. The Commission ensures compliance with FIA regulations, manages scrutineering operations, appoints event officials, and acts as the primary technical authority for competitors participating under the North Macedonian ASN.

🔧 Technical Responsibilities

Oversight of technical scrutineering
Car eligibility and compliance checks
Noise, weight and safety equipment verification
Issuance of technical bulletins and directives
BOP (Balance of Performance) administration

⚖️ Sporting Responsibilities

Appointment of event stewards and officials
Administration of protests and appeals
Liaison with FIA CEZ and promoters
Competitor licensing and eligibility
Issuance of official bulletins and notices
